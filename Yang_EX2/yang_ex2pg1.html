About Us

Real Living Real Estate is a full-service real estate brokerage franchise company with a comprehensive and integrated suite of resources for franchisees and their sales professionals, as well as for consumers who work with them. In 2016 and 2017, Real Living Real Estate was named "Real Estate Agency Brand of the Year" in the ongoing Harris Poll EquiTrend® study; and was named "Most Loved" and "Most Trusted" real estate brand in the study in consecutive years. Additionally, the Real Living Brand and its innovative concepts were recognized by Entrepreneur magazine as well as Inman News, receiving several Inman Innovator Awards. Real Living was also named "the Most Promising New National Brand" by the Swanepoel TRENDS Report. Real Living Real Estate is a network brand of HSF Affiliates LLC, which is owned by HomeServices of America, Inc., a Berkshire Hathaway affiliate.

Real Living leads the real estate industry with a customer satisfaction rating of 98 percent, based upon research conducted by a third party, independent research firm*.

Based in Minneapolis, Minn., HomeServices of America, Inc. is the second-largest, full-service residential real estate brokerage firm and the largest brokerage-owned settlement services provider in the U.S. and is the majority owner in the Prudential Real Estate and Real Living Real Estate franchise networks. Owned by MidAmerican Energy Holdings Company, a consolidated subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway Inc., HomeServices' operating companies offer integrated real estate services, including brokerage services, mortgage originations, title and closing services, property and casualty insurance, home warranties and other homeownership services.

What Makes the Perfect Home?

Finding the perfect home isn’t just about the right price or the best location—it’s about a space that suits your unique needs and lifestyle. And at Real Living Real Estate, we’re here to help you find just that. We understand that everyone’s idea of the perfect home is different, and we’re committed to guiding you to the one that’s made for you. Here’s what we believe truly makes a home perfect:

  1. Right Location
    • Imagine living somewhere that puts everything you need right at your doorstep—whether that’s a short commute to work, proximity to schools, or easy access to parks and recreation. We know the neighborhoods inside and out and will help you find a location that fits your lifestyle.
  2. Layout That Works for You
    • We know that the perfect home is one that feels right as soon as you walk through the door. From spacious living rooms to functional kitchens, we’ll work with you to find a layout that matches how you live, entertain, and unwind.
  3. Space to Grow
    • Whether it’s extra bedrooms for a growing family, a backyard for pets, or an office space for remote work, we’ll help you find a home with the right amount of space to accommodate your current needs—and future plans.
  4. Modern Comforts and Conveniences
    • You deserve a home that makes life easier, with energy-efficient appliances, smart home features, and modern designs that fit your tastes. We’re here to make sure you have everything you need, from convenience to comfort, under one roof.
  5. Security and Safety
    • Your peace of mind is our priority. We’ll make sure you’re in a safe, secure environment, whether that’s through gated communities, smart security features, or a low-crime area, so you can feel confident in your new home.
